# Protocol Roadmap

The following features are planned for future versions of the protocol.

## Multi-Collateral Support

Allow multiple collateral assets.

## Decentralized Price Oracles

Integrate external price feeds for real-time collateral valuation.

## Stability Fees

Introduce dynamic interest rates to stabilize the peg.

## Partial Liquidations

Allow partial liquidation of positions instead of full seizure.

## Governance

Introduce protocol governance for adjusting parameters.

## Advanced Privacy

Improve resistance to off-chain probing attacks.
